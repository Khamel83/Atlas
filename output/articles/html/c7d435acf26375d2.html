You’ll See One-Way Award Pricing for Coach and First Class Alaska Airlines Award Tickets on the Singapore Airlines Award Chart But finding the number of miles you’ll need for an award flight is NOT as simple as finding the price between the zones of your departure and arrival airports.  Because Singapore Airlines does NOT allow transfers (connections) or stopovers on Alaska Airlines awards. Because of this pricing method, there are gaps in the award chart.  These are between zones where NO non-stop Alaska Airlines flights exist, like from the East Coast (Zone 4) to Hawaii or Alaska (Zone 5).  These routes require a connection.
