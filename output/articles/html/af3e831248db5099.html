Though The Business Platinum Card® from American Express has a high annual fee, that $695 (see rates and fees) you'll spend every year you hold the card provides some undeniable perks.

One of those perks is up to $400 in annual statement credits toward Dell purchases made in the U.S (enrollment required). The up to $400 is split up as a statement credit for up to $200 for purchases between January and June and another credit for up to $200 for purchases between July and December.

You must register for the statement credit before using it; it will go to waste if you don't.

This credit will reset July 1, so if you haven't already used it for the first half of the year, time is of the essence.

What won't trigger the credit

Know that Dell doesn't sell its own gift cards directly. You cannot use the credit on international transactions and purchases made via the international contact center, global events, Dell Financial Services, Dell Outlet for Home, Dell Outlet for Work, Dell Partner Direct, Dell Premier, Dell Premier Connect, Dell Premier Select and authorized retailers.

Maximizing your purchase

Before shopping, register for the Dell statement credit. While on the American Express website, check your Amex Offers to see if your Amex Business Platinum is targeted for an Amex Offer on Dell purchases.

The terms may specify that you must make the purchases through dell.com/amex, so you'll probably want to use that link if you're using an Amex Offer. However, if you aren't targeted or if you find a better offer, you can click through a cash-back portal such as Rakuten (which can earn you American Express Membership Rewards points) to maximize your purchase.

Bottom line
